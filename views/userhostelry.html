    Hostelry Reservation Method

    Search, inquire and reserve.

    Search and inquire for your preffered hostelry and reserve. The webiste service is free of charge for users.

    Print receipt

    After you reserve for your preffered hostelry, you must print the receipt for hostelry reservation confirmation

    Confirm and pay

    After you arrive in your reserved hostelry, you must present the receipt. After the hostelry confirmed your reservation, you will pay directly to the counter.
